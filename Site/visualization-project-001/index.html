About the Project

This is an ongoing project in concert with my girlfriend who works at New York Magazine. Since the outbreak of Covid-19 they have been tracking NY mag user sentiment surrounding the events. Using a combination of node.js, a little Python and mostly vanilla javascript, we have been working to visualize the data in unique and compelling ways without abstracting away too much.
